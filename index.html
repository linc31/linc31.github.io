Let's Fly

Team Built Web App

Need to book a flight? Use Let's Fly!
This easy to use app allows travelers to book flights on the fly! Feeling spontaneous? Lets Fly can also choose a random destination for you, and if it's not to your liking feel free to spin again for a new destination.

As a front-end developer, I was responsible for the look and feel of the web app along with the branding. I also had to ensure the technical feasibility of UX/UI designs and optimize the app for maximum speed and scalability.

  • Team: Antonio Navarro, Christina Lin, Christopher De Camp, Nedim Filipovic
  • Technologies Used: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, NodeJS, ExpressJS, MongoDB, Materialize, Google API, Amadeus API, 500px API, Wikipedia API
  • Role:Front-End Developer

ScheduleME

MERN Stack App

ScheduleMe is a web app that allows doctors to create their own schedule and calendar in order to schedule their own patients on a weekly basis. Each doctor is allowed to have their own set of patients to avoid confusion if other doctors decide to use the app as well.

  • Technologies Used: MongoDB, ExpressJS, React, NodeJS, Materialize

BlackJack

Vanilla Javascript

The objective of BlackJack is to get 21 on your first two cards. The game is stocked with one deck of cards, but will reshuffle every 30 cards to avoid card counting. The player will have three standard actions after receiving two initial cards: Stand, Hit, and Deal after the round is finished.

  • Technologies Used: Javascript, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery

Mobile App Design

Vray is a new concept product that allows merchants from retailers to e-commerce to allow customers to purchase their new buys with their fingerprint. It is both for B2B and B2C. My responsibilities were to rework the old user flow and establish a new and more efficient user experience. I also had to restructure the user interface while maintaining the brand consistency.
